Our Story

How it began

Their first date was at Memorial Park in Provo, with sidewalk chalk and the mountains above them. Patrick taught Thea a little economics and multivariable calculus; Thea taught him some physics. Together they drew the Y on the mountain from where they sat, looking up, and watched the clouds drift past. That afternoon set the shape of everything that followed—learning side by side, loving the world around them, and simply being present together.

Their relationship has been filled with study and wonder—science and math, stargazing and temple worship, late nights and steady support. They share a commitment to God and a love for the beauty of creation: clouds, mountains, stars, and the quiet work of faith. They’ve become best friends and partners, ready to serve side by side through the eternities.

Patrick brought Thea back to that same park where it began. He’d covered the sidewalk with chalk drawings of their story—stars and school, families and the mountain they first drew together. They walked slowly, admiring each piece and reflecting on the journey so far. Then Patrick knelt and asked Thea to be side by side with him throughout the eternities. She said, “A thousand times yes!”

They can’t wait to celebrate with you.
